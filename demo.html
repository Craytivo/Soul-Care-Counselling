🚀 How to Get Started

  1. Open the website: Double-click on index.html to open it in your browser
  2. Enable edit mode: Click the "Edit Mode" button in the bottom-right corner
  3. Start editing: Click on any text to edit it directly
  4. Save changes: Press Ctrl+S or click "Save Changes"
