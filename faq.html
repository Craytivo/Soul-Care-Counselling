FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about sessions, rates, policies, and more.

How long will sessions last? +

Each sessions will be for approximately 50 minutes.

Do you only work with faith based clients? +

Although our approach is rooted in faith based principles, we do not only serve faith-based clients. All are welcomed, and we would love to connect with you.

Do you work with clients outside of Canada? +

No, at the moment we only work with clients in Canada.

What is the cost of each session? +

Each session will cost $160.00. Student rates are available for high school and undergraduate university students. Please email for further details.

Do I need a referral? +

You do not need a referral in order for us to journey together, you are able to book a free consultation here; after which we will discuss next steps.

Where are you located? +

Soul Care Christian counselling offers virtual sessions online through secure tele-health platforms.

Do I have to commit to a certain amount of sessions? +

Commitment is an important and necessary aspect of the healing journey; however, each person's healing and soul care journey is different, so your commitment will also look different. As the client, you have the agency to define what commitment looks like for you.

What is your cancellation policy? +

Cancellations between a 24-48 hour period will require 50% of the session fee. If a cancellation is made on the day of (within the 24 hours leading up to the appointment), full session payment will be required.

What types of payments do you accept? +

We accept credit, debit, and interact e-transfers.
